Our Mission


To date, we’ve led programs around the world in 20+ cities, from Brazil to Qatar, helping students everywhere build the confidence and curiosity to explore STEM through building. This global reach reflects our core belief: access to opportunity shouldn't depend on where you're from — or how traditional classrooms made you feel.
